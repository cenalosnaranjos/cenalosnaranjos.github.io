CETA "Los Naranjos"

Welcome to the EVA System! Here's how to use it:

  • Access: Enter your unique access code on the login screen.
  • Personalized Content: Once inside, you'll see content tailored to your level.
  • Connectivity: Find scheduled virtual meetings with direct links.
  • Class Recordings: Watch past class recordings, filtered by week.
  • Study Material: Access PDFs for your study and practice.
  • Activities Platform (A1): If you are an A1 student, access additional interactive activities.
  • Teacher Dashboard (Teachers): If you are a teacher, view student lists and content overview by level.
  • Logout: Use the "Logout" button to exit the system.
  • WhatsApp Support: Click the WhatsApp button to chat directly with your teacher for doubts.
  • Technical Help: Use the headset button for technical assistance.

If you have any further questions, please contact our support team.
